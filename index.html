Why inSCIgnis?

Essence of science as said by Sir C.V. Raman is independent thinking and not equipment and the prime objective of inSCIgnis is to foster this independent thinking through scientific temperament among young minds to fuel up their thinking towards creating a better society and a nation in general. The objective revolves around giving exposure to underprivileged students to the amazing world of science through various events and helping them explore new opportunities. It develops a guiding light among students and encourages them to pursue science as a subject to contribute to national development.

Event full of fun and science

Back in the year 2014, the students and authorities of Tezpur University sowed the seeds of a grand science fest and coined it 'inSCIgnis’. Revealing a mosaic of elating and interesting events, the university, with the joined endeavours from the Tezpur University Students' Science Council (TUSSC) and the authority commended the celebration 11 years ago, which has now become one of the biggest and most compelling science festivals in the whole North Eastern region of the country. Interestingly, it has witnessed its growth as rapid as seen in an insignis plant. Over time, it has arisen as a platform, where science, in its valid quintessence, is praised and encouraged.
